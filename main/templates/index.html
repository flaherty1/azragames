What is the Genesis Collection?

The Genesis Collection is Azra Games’ first NFT Mint. Owning these PFPs (Play Forever Passes) is the only way to get access to perpetual utility and exclusive rewards in every game we release. Join the future.

Who are the characters?

The characters in this collection are Genesis Explorers - intrepid space adventurers that hold a unique role in the lore of our first game, Project Arcanas.

When will the Genesis Collection and its staking system release?

The collection will be released in two parts later this year. Both parts of the collection are distinct in the exclusive rewards they provide. The staking and gamification of Genesis Explorers will roll out shortly after the first mint.

How do they provide perpetual utility in every game?

Genesis Explorers can be staked in Project Arcanas for exclusive rewards used to craft and sell to other players. The gamification of Genesis Explorers is integral to the core loop of Project Arcanas, and will never become obsolete as future game experiences roll out. Owning these PFPs will also grant access to exclusive rewards in future games.

Why join the Azra Games Community?

At Azra Games, we have a rule: no mini-games. We believe in taking the time to release quality experiences that fit together perfectly as a whole. We also believe in crafting systems and economies that will provide value to both passive and active players for years to come. We can’t wait to share more about the Genesis Collection and Project Arcanas with you soon.
